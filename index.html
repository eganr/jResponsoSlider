jResponsoSlider

A jQuery responsive slider plugin

Yep it's another slider

But it's a responsive! And features touch events! Yaaaaay!

And it doesn't really slide, if you want to get technical.

Markup Required

<ul class="jrs-slider">
  <li>
    <img src="pic1.jpg" alt="Picture of a horse" />
  </li>
  <li>
    <img src="pic2.jpg" alt="Not a picture of a horse" />
  </li>
  <li>
    <img src="pic3.jpg" alt="Picture of a horse" />
  </li>
</ul>

Usage

$('.jrs-slider').jResponsoSlider();

That's just about it!
